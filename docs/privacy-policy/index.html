QR Code Generator

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what happens to information when you use the QR Code Generator Canva app (the "App"). The short version: the App is a thin client that runs inside Canva. It does not have its own backend, does not collect personal information, and does not transmit your inputs to any third-party server other than Canva itself.

1. What the App collects

The App collects the following inputs you provide while using it:

  • The URL you type into the URL field.
  • The foreground and background colors you choose.
  • The transparent-background and quiet-zone toggles.

These inputs are used only to generate the QR code SVG locally inside the Canva iframe. The App does not maintain its own server and does not log, store, or transmit your inputs anywhere outside Canva.

2. How the QR code reaches your design

To insert the generated QR code into your Canva design, the App calls Canva's official asset-upload and design-element APIs. Canva receives the rendered SVG so it can place it on your canvas. Your use of Canva is governed by Canva's Privacy Policy.

3. What the App does not do

  • It does not use cookies or local storage to track you.
  • It does not include analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
  • It does not contact any third-party server other than Canva.
  • It does not access your designs, files, or account information beyond what is necessary to insert the QR code element you explicitly request.

4. Permissions

The App requests the canva:design:content:write scope from Canva so that it can insert the QR code into your active design. It does not request read access to your design content.

5. Children

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from them.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
